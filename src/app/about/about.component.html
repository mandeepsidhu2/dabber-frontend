Manage the traffic into the parking system

Tabber is a part of the suvidham ecosystem, to manage and collect data of the various types of vehicles that enter into our parking system.The data is carefully assessed to reallocate or disallocate parking space to a particular kind of vehicle in order to function with the best usage of the space available.This also keeps a record of the number of vehicles processed by each employee and the category of each.
There is a chat communication system for the employees to communicate any kind of issues or tag a concerned employee.The employee identity is managed through google authentication to prevent any untoward updates in the database.Only authenticated employees may communicate
Tabber strives to improve the user experience by improving the efficiency in space and management of the parking system.

Features offered by Tabber

Check the stats for an employee Search by name or email,see stats for previous days Check the vehicles counted The vehicles counted by an employee datewise, visualised in graphs Update the count Update whenever a new vehicle enters the system Chat with other employees Report issues, or convey important messages
